All over! Allosaurus lived in the Jurassic period and inhabited Africa, Europe and North America. Its fossils have been found in places such as Utah, North Rhine-Westphalia (Germany) and Nebraska.

Meat! And lots of it. Allosaurus were very efficient hunters with jaws that could open incredibly wide and huge, serrated teeth. Experts are not sure if they only hunted live prey, or if they would sometimes scavenge dead prey as well. Either way, they were built to be effective hunters; I would have been very scared to be a more docile herbivore when the Allosaurus was around.
